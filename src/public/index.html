Tip Of The Day Bot for Slack

Share Small Bits Of Knowledge With Your Team, daily.

Usage

1. Add to your slack room

Click the Add to Slack button, pick a room where you want to activate the bot and you're good to go.

2. Add Tips

Everyone in the channel can add some tips via /tipoftheday-add You can use X to archieve Y.

3. Daily Tips

Every day one random tip will posted inside your slack room.
